Design Choices

Recurring Patterns
The highest-value section. Spots things you wouldn't notice in individual sessions — repeated friction, habits, tool gaps.
Improvement Proposals with frequency
Aggregates suggestions from session journals. An idea that surfaces 3x across weeks is screaming to be acted on.
Thread Progress as narrative
Not just a list of sessions — one-line narrative per thread. "How's my week going?" at a glance.
Voice: yours, not a report
The skill will include voice calibration from your actual writing samples so journals sound like you, not an AI summary.
